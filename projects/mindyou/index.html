Mind You

Helping you manage your mental health

- Overview -

Problem

The United Nations Division for Social Policy and Development reports that one in four people around the globe will experience a mental health condition in their lifetime. These conditions impede the ability of people to concentrate, perform daily tasks, and interact with others. The expense of treatment and stigmas surrounding mental health prevent more than half of people from pursuing treatment according to the World Health Organization.

Solution

Tracking factors about your lifestyle may help you pinpoint what affects your mental health. If you need additional support beyond independent tracking, connecting with a therapist may assist you in finding relief from your symptoms. Our mobile and watch apps provide a platform for you to track your mental health and find a therapist.
